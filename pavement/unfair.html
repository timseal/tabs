------

minor.

For the part after the break, just fool around with the higher octaves and
notes. You can hear the bass playing extra notes too at the outro, if you
want to play them on the guitar they're E(play open E then slide to 12th
fret), A, and B

Like I said, this isn't exact because there might be more to the chords
than what i put here, and Malkmus is probably tuned alternately, but these
are pretty much the correct notes.
