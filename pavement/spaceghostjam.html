space ghost jam

by Pavement (aka "The Beatles") on "Space Ghost: Coast to Coast"
You can get an mp3 of the song from Steve Krotosky's Pavement Online
Archive http://udel.edu/~krotosky/pavement.html  (at the time I made this
tab)

Nice little jam song, real simple. NOTICE that it's in DADABE tuning,
which SM seems to use a lot.
