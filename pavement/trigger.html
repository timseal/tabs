tabbed by Trent Paton (trentus@hotmail.com)

This is such an incredible song and probably my all time favourite
Pavement song. I'm surprised it hasn't been tabbed before...anyway,
here it is...
INTRO
I just play downstrokes on a G barre chord, lifting my index finger
and playing an upstroke every 8th strum. I doubt that it's actually
played that way but it sounds ok if you're just playing the song for
a singalong or whatever. Also, only strum the lowest 3 or 4 strings...
that goes for the rest of the song as well.
