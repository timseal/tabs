I'm so pissed off at how nothing is happening at the pavement websites that I decided to post these guitar tunings. I've been looking at the old pavement guide and I agreed with some of it but found a lot of false things. I'd really like these tunings to be put up if you don't mind because I know they are pretty accurate and make a lot of sense. I have been playing for a very long time so I know what I'm doing. One day when I have nothing to do I might post the catalog of tabs I have with the correct way to play them. But I'm not going to waste my time if no one will post them. I've been studying malkmus during his performances and seeing how he has been playing these songs. The only tunings I use are the ones that come out of his mouth.
