        Well, I've bought the "new" (actually, it's a EMI compilation) 
R.E.M. album, and I heard that "Time After Time/Red Rain/So. Central Rain" 
live medley that contains a really nice version of "So. Central Rain". 
It's just Michael singing and Peter playing.

        To play this version, just replace the C on the bass solo:
   
          e --0---------------
          B ----1-----1-------
          G ------------(0)---
          D ------0h2---------
          A -3--------------3-
   
        and the G on the other bass solo:

          e ---3---------
          B -----3-------
          G -----------0-
          D -------------
          A -------0h2---
          E -3-----------
            (actually, Peter doesn't play this way, but I think
             this way is OK, too!)

        Play the other chords in harmonics.
